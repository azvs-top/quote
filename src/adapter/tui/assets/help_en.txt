List
  j/k or Down/Up  move selection
  J/K             jump last/first item in page
  n/p or Right/Left next/prev page
  H/L             first/last page
  l or Enter      open detail
  r               reload page

Detail
  j/k or Down/Up  scroll detail
  h               back to list

Global
  g               open goto popup
  :               open command mode

Goto Popup
  j/k or Down/Up  choose page
  Enter           jump
  Esc or g        close

Command
  :help           open help
  :q or :quit     quit
  Esc             cancel input

Help
  t               toggle help language
